Over 30 years

Busy Bee has consistently been voted the top child care and education center on the South Shore by Reader's Choice Awards. Since opening in 1989, we have focused on cognitive, emotional, motor development, identifying developmental delays, and providing resources to help your child thrive!

Experienced Educators

Meet our experienced Busy Bee childcare staff. Teachers are EEC licensed and certified. Prior to hiring, all staff have completed background checks including: CORI, SORI, DCF & Fingerprinting. Our training program exceeds the EEC Training required by the State of Massachusetts and is committed to the success and preparedness for each and every child cared for by our teachers. Staff members receive First Aid, CPR, and Rescue Medication training. Not only are our teachers highly trained and knowledgeable, they also possess a true passion to help your child grow!

Safety First

Your child's safety is our number one priority. Our center doors are always locked and students are signed in and out by both teachers and parents. We conduct Role Call at key points throughout our day, each classroom is video monitored, and there are daily safety checks of the classrooms. We are also the only center on the South Shore with an alert button that contacts local safety officials in case of an emergency. It is of utmost importance that Busy Bee Childcare is a secure environment for all students and staff.

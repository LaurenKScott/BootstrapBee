Teachers

Busy Bee's success has been built over the past 30+ years by its outstanding teachers. With many of them teaching at Busy Bee for over 15 years, they have obtained the knowledge and qualities to make your child's first school experience a memorable one.

All staff have completed background checks including: CORI, SORI, DCF & Fingerprinting and they also receive First Aid, CPR, and Rescue Medication training. These trainings are ongoing with both EEC and Busy Bee regulations. Our policy and practicum training is offered in-house by veteran teachers. Assistants undergo training as well, and are under the supervision of certified staff members. Each teacher and aide must meet and maintain all requirements by EEC and Busy Bee to remain a part of our team. We strive to keep our staff well educated with the changing of times and believe that old-school values complimented with newer teaching styles helps create the perfect balance within our classrooms. Our staff strives to establish and maintain a positive, encouraging relationship with students and families. They possess all of the qualities that make our center the best!
